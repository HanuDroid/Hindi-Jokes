Getting Started

Thankyou for downloading this app.
This application is very user friendly and does not need any help.

However, for your reference, we have documented some information that you may need.

Navigation:

Just swipe to right / left on the screen to navigate to next / previous joke.
Switch to landscape layout to access a list of all jokes at a time.

Notifications about new jokes:

We updates jokes on daily basis. Whenever a new joke is posted, we will notify. In the Jelly Bean version, you can expand the notifications to see the title of the jokes.

Submit Jokes:

Share your jokes with your friends by submitting a joke in the app. Find the menu option to upload a new joke. The jokes will be reviewed by the moderators before they are published.
Once the joke is approved / rejected, the user will be notified.

Memes:

You can get Memes (Graphical images) also. The images are stored in internal storage space.
Memes older than 7 days are automatically deleted to optimize storage space.
If you do not wish to get memes, you can deactivate them in settings screen.

Report bugs

To request a new feature or to report a bug in the app, please write Email to the developer.
